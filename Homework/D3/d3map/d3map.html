Population of the world

For this visualisation, I added a mouseover-effect to the map (via geography.popupTemplate) with live population data from restcountries.eu. I made sure to divide the countries in fairly equal categories (in total country size), except for the largest countries. For this, I used the chroma YlGnBu-scale, to make sure that the colors can be read in their correct order. This map allows the user to globally explore the world, and see where the large countries (and small countries) are, and compare the populations of two countries.
